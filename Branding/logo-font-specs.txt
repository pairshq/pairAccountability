LOGO FONT SPECIFICATIONS FOR CANVA
====================================

Original Font Stack:
- system-ui, -apple-system, sans-serif
- On macOS: San Francisco (SF Pro)

Canva Alternatives (in order of similarity):
1. Inter (BEST MATCH - modern, clean, similar to San Francisco)
2. Helvetica Neue
3. Arial
4. Proxima Nova

Typography Settings:
-------------------
Font Weight: Regular (400)
Letter Spacing: -0.5px (slightly tighter/condensed)
Font Size: 32px (for horizontal logo)
Font Style: Normal (no italic/bold)

Color Specifications:
--------------------
Text Color (on white background): #1E293B
Text Color (on dark background): #FFFFFF
Background Color (dark): #1E293B

Logo Dimensions:
---------------
Icon: 100x100 viewBox
Horizontal Logo: Icon + "pair" text side by side
Stacked Logo: Icon above "pair" text

Icon Details:
------------
- "P" shape with CIRCULAR/ROUNDED top (not sharp corners)
- The top part forms a smooth circular curve using quadratic bezier curves
- Vertical stem on the left
- Two dots below (at y=68, x=42 and x=52)
- Stroke width: 3.5
- Rounded line caps and joins
- The circular top goes from x=25 to x=65, with curves at y=20, 35, and 50
